About Us

Welcome to Colette, the café the doesn’t just do food, it makes your dining experience memorable.

As the city’s first eatery to introduce savory crêpes, Colette has become a trusted favorite for those who crave flavor, variety, and warmth. The menu is generous, from breakfast to burgers and signature seafood dishes.

Step into Colette and you’re greeted by a cost, inviting ambiance (Oh, don’t forget to grab a picture with our signature tree).
